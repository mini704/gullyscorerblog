Step 1: Setting up the Digital Dashboard

Organizing a cricket tournament requires keeping track of dozens of players and matches. By using a web app like Gully Scorer, you can create an admin hub on your mobile browser. No heavy app downloads required for you or the teams.

Step 2: Onboarding Teams and Squads

When you start a new match, simply type in the team names. Gully Scorer automatically remembers your team squads. You can add "Free Agents" or fully verified players. Once a team plays their first match, their roster is saved to the cloud, making future match setups take only 5 seconds.

Step 3: Tracking Tournament Progress

As matches finish, you don't need to manually calculate Net Run Rates or update standings graphics. Simply go to the Points Table section, select the matches in your current tournament, and the app instantly generates a shareable image showing Rankings, Wins, Losses, and NRR.
