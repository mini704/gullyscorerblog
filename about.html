About Gully Scorer

Our Mission

Gully Scorer is a free cricket scoring platform created to help local cricket players, organizers, schools, clubs, and communities manage matches professionally. Our goal is to make digital cricket scoring simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.

What We Offer

  • Live cricket scoring
  • Tournament management
  • Team and player management
  • Match history
  • Points table and Net Run Rate (NRR)
  • DLS calculator
  • Mobile-friendly web application

Why We Built Gully Scorer

Many local cricket tournaments still use paper scorebooks, making it difficult to manage matches and maintain player records. Gully Scorer was created to solve these problems with an easy-to-use digital platform.

Our Vision

We aim to become one of the most trusted digital cricket scoring platforms for local and grassroots cricket.
