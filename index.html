Bringing Professional Scoring to Every Match

Cricket is not just a sport in India. It is an emotion. From narrow streets and school grounds to village tournaments and local leagues, millions of matches are played every day. Every player remembers the sixes they hit, the wickets they took, and the matches they won. But most of these memories disappear because there is no proper way to record them.

This very problem inspired the creation of Gully Scorer, designed to help local players and organizers manage matches professionally with live scoring, tournament management, statistics, and analytics.

The Problem with Traditional Gully Cricket Scoring

For years, local cricket matches have been scored using notebooks, rough papers, or simple memory. While this works for casual games, it creates several major challenges:

  • Scores can be forgotten easily.
  • Vital match statistics are completely lost.
  • Players cannot track their long-term performance.
  • Tournament organizers struggle to manage standings and run rates.
  • There is no professional experience for grassroots players.

In many tournaments, disagreements happen because scores are not recorded properly. Players often argue about runs, wickets, and match results. As the sport becomes more competitive at the grassroots level, there is a growing need for a simple and digital solution.

Key Features of Gully Scorer

Gully Scorer is a modern platform designed specifically for street games, turf games, college tournaments, and community leagues. The goal is simple: Make every local match feel professional.

Live Cricket Scoring

Users can score matches ball by ball with an easy interface. Every run, wicket, extra, and over is recorded instantly, helping teams maintain accurate match records without the hassle.

Team & Player Management

Players can create teams and manage squad details effortlessly. This helps tournament organizers and captains keep everything organized in one centralized place.

DLS Calculator Integration

Rain interruptions often affect local matches. Our app includes a powerful DLS target calculation feature, allowing fair target adjustments during interrupted games.

Tournament Management

Managing local tournaments is difficult. We simplify it by helping organizers maintain match schedules, team standings, points tables, and overall tournament progress.

Professional Match History & Scorecards

Every match becomes a permanent record. Players can revisit previous performances, analyze their growth, and track their improvement over time.

Instead of handwritten records, users receive detailed digital scorecards that can be instantly shared via WhatsApp, Facebook, or Instagram with teammates, friends, and participants.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Gully Scorer completely free?

Yes! The core platform for live scoring and tournament management is entirely free for local players and organizers.

Can I manage an entire tournament?

Absolutely. You can manage teams, schedules, fixtures, and auto-generating points tables with Net Run Rate (NRR) calculations.

Can I score matches offline?

While it requires an internet connection for live sharing, the app is highly optimized to load fast and use minimal data even on slow mobile networks.

How many players can I add?

There is no strict limit. You can add large squads for various teams participating in your tournament.

Can I share the live score with others?

Yes! Every match generates a unique link that you can share. Viewers can follow the match ball-by-ball from anywhere.

Can I edit the score if I make a mistake?

Yes, the scorer has the ability to edit past deliveries and correct mistakes such as wide balls, no-balls, or run allocations.

Does it work well on mobile phones?

Yes, it is a mobile-first platform. It works beautifully on both Android and iOS browsers without needing to download a large app from the store.

Does it calculate Net Run Rate (NRR)?

Yes, tournament points tables automatically update Net Run Rate (NRR) after every match concludes.
